About the Game

VoxelCraft is an original, open-ended sandbox game built entirely from blocks. You spawn into a procedurally generated world that stretches as far as you care to walk, made of grass, stone, oceans, deserts, forests and snowy mountains. Dig straight down into cave systems, gather ores, craft tools and gear, and reshape the landscape one block at a time.

But VoxelCraft is more than just digging and building. The world is alive and dangerous: volcanoes erupt and pour lava down their slopes, earthquakes tear open the ground, floods sweep across the land, and sprawling cities buzz with traffic and wandering citizens. When night falls, hostile creatures come out — and seven different dimensions wait beyond glowing portals for those brave enough to travel.

It runs right in your browser with nothing to install, on both computer and phone.

Features

Endless Worlds

Procedurally generated terrain with biomes, caves, oceans and over a hundred block types to build with.

7 Dimensions

Build portals and travel to fiery, frozen, shadowy and radiant realms — each with its own look and creatures.

Living Cities

Explore generated cities with streets, shops, traffic and citizens who go about their day — and flee when disaster strikes.

Natural Disasters

Erupting volcanoes with ash and lava flows, earthquakes, and rising floods that reshape the world.

Abilities & Magic

Earn experience, level up and unlock skills — fireballs, healing, lightning, dashing, gliding and more.

Creatures & Bosses

Friendly animals, nighttime monsters, dimension-specific enemies and a roaming boss to challenge.

How to Play

On Computer

MoveW forward, S back, Q left, D right
LookMove the mouse (click the game first to lock the cursor)
Jump / Fly upA or Space
Mine / PlaceLeft-click to break a block, right-click to place
HotbarNumber keys 1–9, or scroll
MenusL abilities, K warp, X special sight
DisastersG earthquake, J flood

On Phone / Tablet

Use the on-screen joystick (bottom-left) to move and drag the right side of the screen to look around. Tap the right side to mine the block you're aiming at. The buttons let you place blocks, jump, fly, cast spells, open menus, and trigger earthquakes and floods.

Getting Started

Punch trees and stone to gather materials, open the crafting menu to make tools and useful blocks, and build a shelter before night. Then start exploring — find a volcano, build a portal to another dimension, or just build something amazing.

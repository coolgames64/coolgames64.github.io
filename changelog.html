Updates & Changelog

VoxelCraft is updated regularly. Here's what has been added and improved over time, newest first.

Living World Update

June 2026
  • Cities now have moving traffic and citizens who wander the streets, duck into buildings, and flee when disaster strikes.
  • Volcanoes now actively erupt — launching lava bombs, raining ash and embers, and sending lava flows down their slopes.
  • Added rush-hour traffic that rises and falls with the in-game time of day.
  • Earthquakes and floods can now strike on their own, not just on command.
  • Major performance work for smoother movement and lighter load on phones and tablets.

Disasters & Cities Update

June 2026
  • Added volcanoes — towering basalt cones with lava-filled craters.
  • Added generated cities with street grids, shops and tall buildings.
  • Added earthquakes (screen shake + ground fissures) and floods (rising, then receding water).
  • Added the new Aetheria sky dimension of radiant floating islands.

Sandbox Tools Update

May 2026
  • Added the Nuke block, Black Hole egg and Tsunami egg for large-scale world effects.
  • Added quicksand that you sink into and have to struggle out of.
  • The black hole now pulls in the player, creatures and terrain as spiralling debris.

Mobile & Controls Update

May 2026
  • Added full touch controls: an analog joystick, drag-to-look and tap-to-mine.
  • Added on-screen buttons for placing, jumping, flying, spells and menus.
  • Improved device detection so phones and tablets run the lighter, smoother profile.

Abilities & Dimensions Update

April 2026
  • Added the Aeon Forge ability system with four skill trees and active spells.
  • Added multiple dimensions reachable through craftable portals.
  • Added procedural sound effects for mining, placing, combat and spells.
  • Added more creatures, including dimension-specific enemies and a boss.

Initial Release

Early 2026
  • Infinite procedurally generated world with biomes, caves and oceans.
  • Mining, crafting, building and a day/night cycle.
  • Over a hundred block types and a working inventory.

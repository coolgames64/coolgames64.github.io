Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VoxelCraft ("we", "us", "the site") handles information when you visit and play our game. By using the site you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

VoxelCraft does not require an account and we do not ask you for personal information such as your name or address. The information involved falls into two groups:

  • Game data stored on your own device. Your progress, inventory, settings and graphics preference are saved in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Standard technical data. Like most websites, our hosting and our advertising partners may automatically receive technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, for security, analytics and advertising.

2. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We and our partners use them to remember your preferences, measure traffic, and serve advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as well.

3. Google AdSense & advertising

This site is supported by advertising served through Google AdSense. To provide ads, Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies at www.aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu.

For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how the game is used (for example, which features are popular) so we can improve it. This data is aggregated and not used to identify you personally.

5. Consent for EU/UK/EEA visitors (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we and our advertising partners rely on your consent to use cookies for personalised advertising. Where required, you will be shown a consent message and can accept, reject, or manage your choices. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies or using the opt-out links above.

6. California privacy rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's privacy

VoxelCraft is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Your choices

  • Clear your saved game and preferences by clearing your browser's site data for this site.
  • Block or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • Opt out of personalised ads using the links in section 3.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

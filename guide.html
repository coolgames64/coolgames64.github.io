How to Play VoxelCraft

New here? This guide covers everything you need to start mining, crafting and building in your infinite world.

Controls (computer)

  • Move: W / S / D and the mouse to look around.
  • Jump / swim up: A.
  • Sprint / fast fly: hold Ctrl.
  • Break a block: left click. Place a block: right click.
  • Choose a block: number keys 1–9 or scroll the mouse wheel.
  • Fly on/off: F (then A to go up, B to go down).
  • Menus: E inventory · M market · P dressing room · C crafting.
  • Graphics quality: use the Graphics button on the menu — pick Low for the smoothest performance or High to see further.

Controls (phone & tablet)

  • Left joystick to move, drag the right side of the screen to look.
  • Tap the mine and place buttons to break and build.
  • On-screen buttons handle jump, fly and the menus.

Mining & resources

Break blocks to collect them. Dig underground to find coal, iron, gold and diamond. Ores can be turned into coins in the Add-on Market, and into tools and blocks in Crafting.

Crafting & building

Open Crafting (C) to turn raw materials into planks, bricks and decorative blocks. Switch on Creative Mode (R) for unlimited blocks if you just want to build.

Tips for beginners

  • If the game ever feels slow, set Graphics to Low from the menu.
  • Build a shelter before night — it's safer.
  • Explore caves for the best ores, but watch out for lava.
  • Your progress saves automatically in your browser.

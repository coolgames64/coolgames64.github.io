🎯 How to Play

Your complete guide to playing games on Cool Games 64

🏠 Getting Started

Playing games on our website is super easy! Just follow these simple steps:

1

Browse Our Game Collection

Scroll through our homepage to see all available games. We have action, puzzle, racing, and more!

2

Use Categories to Filter

Click buttons like "Action", "Puzzle", "Racing" to see only games in that category.

3

Search for a Specific Game

Type the game name in the search bar at the top to find it instantly!

4

Click to Play!

Click on any game card and it will open immediately in your browser. No downloads needed!

Each game uses different controls. Check the game description on our site for the controls, or look at the instructions on the game page itself. Look for "Press SPACE to Start" or similar prompts!

📱 Mobile & Touch Controls

No keyboard? No problem! Our games work on phones and tablets with touch controls:

📱

Touch D-Pad Buttons

On games like Racing and Snake, you'll see ◀ ▲ ▶ buttons at the bottom of your screen. Tap them to move!

👆

Tap to Click

For click-based games (bubble shooter, puzzle games), just tap anywhere on the screen!

👆👆

Swipe to Move

In some games, swipe in any direction to control movement. Try Snake - swipe to change direction!

💡 Pro Tip: On mobile, rotate your phone to landscape mode for a better gaming experience!

About VoxelCraft

VoxelCraft is a free, original voxel sandbox game that runs entirely in your web browser — no download, no account, no install. It started as a passion project and has grown into a full world of mining, building, exploration, magic, multiple dimensions and dramatic natural disasters.

What it is

At its heart, VoxelCraft is about creativity and exploration. You shape an endless, procedurally generated world block by block: dig deep for ore, build whatever you can imagine, and venture out to discover volcanoes, generated cities, and portals leading to other realms. An ability system lets you grow stronger over time, and a living world full of weather, creatures and disasters keeps every session different.

How it was made

The game is built with standard web technology — HTML, JavaScript and the Three.js 3D library — so it runs on practically any modern device, from desktop computers to phones and tablets. The entire world is generated procedurally from code, which is why it can stretch on forever and why no two worlds look the same. It is developed and maintained by a solo hobbyist who enjoys building games and sharing them for free.

Free to play

VoxelCraft is free for everyone. The free version is supported by ads, which help cover the cost of hosting and keep the game free to play. If you'd prefer an ad-free experience, there's an optional one-time purchase that removes ads — but it's entirely optional, and the full game is available to everyone at no cost.

Contact & feedback

Feedback, bug reports and ideas are always welcome. You can reach the developer through the project's GitHub page at github.com/coolgames64. Thanks for playing!

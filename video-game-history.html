Have You Ever Wondered...

How did video games start? Who invented them? And how did we go from simple black-and-white games to amazing 3D worlds? Let's travel back in time and find out! 🚀

$200+ Billion

1958

🎯 The First Video Game Ever

A scientist named William Higinbotham created "Tennis for Two" on a HUGE computer that filled an entire room! It was just two lines and a dot, but it amazed everyone who saw it. No one owned it like today - it was just a demonstration!

🤯 Cool Fact!

The computer it ran on had less power than your pocket calculator!

1972

🏓 Pong - The Game That Started It All

Atari released Pong - a simple game of table tennis. Two paddles, one ball, and that's it! But people went CRAZY for it! This was the first commercially successful video game. Soon, Pong machines were in every arcade and bar!

🏓

Pong

1972 - The beginning!

1977

👾 Space Invaders Arrives!

Space Invaders from Japan changed EVERYTHING! For the first time, enemies moved and got harder as you shot them! It cost only $110 to make, and Japan made $450 MILLION from it! This proved video games could be a real business.

🎮 Fun Fact!

Space Invaders was so popular in Japan that the country had a shortage of 100-yen coins!

1980

🕹️ Pac-Man Takes Over the World

A Japanese woman named Toru Iwatani created Pac-Man! She designed it with her wife's help. It was the first video game with a REAL character you could root for! Pac-Man even got a hit song - "Pac-Man Fever"! Over 7 million arcade machines were sold!

🔴 Mario and Nintendo Revolution

Nintendo released the NES with Super Mario Bros! This was like nothing anyone had seen before - colorful graphics, actual music, and a story! The game was so good it SAVED the video game industry after a crash in 1983!

🎩 The 3D Revolution

Games went from flat 2D to REAL 3D worlds! Sony PlayStation and Nintendo 64 brought games into your living room with incredible 3D graphics. Final Fantasy 7, Mario 64, and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time were groundbreaking!

🥽 Virtual Reality and Beyond!

Now we have VR headsets like Quest 3 that let you STEP INSIDE games! Games can be played anywhere with cloud gaming. The future is here - and it's only getting more amazing!

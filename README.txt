VOXELCRAFT — WEBSITE FILES
==========================

These are all the files for your live website. Upload EVERY file in this
folder to the ROOT of your domain (the same place your current index.html
lives), keeping the same names.

FILES
  index.html      -> the game (this REPLACES your old index.html)
  about.html      -> About page
  contact.html    -> Contact page   ** edit the email address before uploading **
  privacy.html    -> Privacy Policy  (required for AdSense)
  terms.html      -> Terms of Use
  guide.html      -> How to play
  changelog.html  -> Updates
  ads.txt         -> AdSense ads.txt (must sit at yourdomain.com/ads.txt)

BEFORE YOU UPLOAD
  1. Open contact.html and privacy.html and replace
     REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_EMAIL@example.com with your real email.

AFTER YOU UPLOAD
  2. Check these all open in a browser:
       yourdomain.com/privacy.html
       yourdomain.com/ads.txt   (should show one line of text)
  3. In your AdSense dashboard, turn ON "Auto ads" for the site.
  4. Wait for Google's review to finish. Ads appear automatically once approved.

NOTES
  - The game name is VoxelCraft (NOT "Minecraft") to avoid trademark problems
    that get AdSense sites rejected.
  - The game loads its 3D engine (three.js) from a CDN, so visitors need to be
    online (normal for a website).
